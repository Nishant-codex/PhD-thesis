\section{Research Data Management}
All data used in this thesis is freely available on the respective data repositories which are adequately referred in appropriate places. The scripts for analysis and figures in each chapter are available as openly accessible repository at the following links: 
\begin{itemize}
    \item \bf{Chapter 2}: \url{https://github.com/Nishant-codex/single_cell_analysis}
    \item \bf{Chapter 3}: \url{https://github.com/Nishant-codex/single_cell_analysis} 
    \item \bf{Chapter 4}: \url{https://github.com/Nishant-codex/DDN-public} 
\end{itemize}

The  research  has  followed  all  applicable  laws  and  ethical  guidelines.  Research  Data  Management  was performed according to the FAIR principles.

\section{Ethical approval}
All experimental work, as described in the articles cited, was carried out in accordance with the European directive 2010/63/EU, the Dutch national regulations and international standards for animal care and use.
